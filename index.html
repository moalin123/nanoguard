What is NanoGuard?

NanoGuard is a smart, eco-friendly mask designed to protect you with advanced filters, health sensors, and self-cleaning technology - all at once

Core Features - How does it work?

  • Nanoparticle Biosensors: Detect pathogens in the air at a microscopic level
  • Electrochemical Sensors: Measures air temperature, humidity and air pressure in real time - especially helpful for those with respiratory conditions
  • Bluetooth Module: Sends health data securely to your device in real time.
  • Graphene or Copper-Infused Filter: Neutralises germs and improves air quality.

How is it powered?

Our proposal for future NanoGuard models: a mask that runs on clean, wearable energy - no bulky batteries

  • Motion Power (Piezo/Triebo-electric): Converts breathing & face movement into energy.
  • Solar Strip: Thin-film solar cells recharge it in light.
  • Lithium Polymer Battery: To aid in case of increased demand/lack of movement
  • Energy Stored: In a micro-capacitor that powers the sensors + Bluetooth.

Cost & Accessibility:

For NanoGuard, not only do we value innovation and creativity, we also consider affordability - which is we are introducing two models:

  • Standard Everday Use: Budget friendly with key protection feautures like copper-based antimicrobial properties for self cleaning.
  • Surgical Use: Full smart features, including graphene infused filters for enhanced durability and advanced sensors designed for the medical environment.

Both models are lightweight, reusable and designed for your peace of mind
